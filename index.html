Welcome to Disc Golf DC

About Disc Golf DC

Disc Golf DC is a Non-profit dedicated to bringing a thriving disc golf community to DC.

About the founders

Ryan Preston the mastermind behind the organization along with his trusty sidekick Christian Halsey had a dream to bring disc golf to the District by creating a vibrant community centered around a Disc golf course and clubhouse in Rock Creek Park.

How to become a disc golf player

  1. Buy a disc
  2. Throw this disc as much as possible
  3. Buy another disc
  4. Throw this disc as much as possible
  5. Continue doing this until you are out of money and there is no space left in your home
  6. You have mastered disc golf
